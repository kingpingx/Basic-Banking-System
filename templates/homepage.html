The Coders Bank is a financial institution which performs the deposit and lending function. A bank allows a person with excess money (Saver) to deposit his money in the bank and earns an interest rate. Similarly, the bank lends to a person who needs money (investor/borrower) at an interest rate. The bank usually takes a deposit from the public at a much lower rate called deposit rate and lends the money to the borrower at a higher interest rate called lending rate.
The Coders Bank, as a full service universal bank, provides a wide gamut of financial products and services encompassing deposits, loans, payment services and investment solutions.
